About Me


My name is Leah O'Gorman. I am a Canadian/American who currently lives in New Jersey, USA. I am an artist with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Sculpture from Tyler School of Art in Philadelphia PA. My work generally falls under mixed media installation work.

Outside of Fine Art, I also majored in theatre with a focus on fabrication. I have used this knowledge to work in theatre, concerts, events, television, and film. Most recently, while living in Vancouver, I worked as a Welder's Assistant and Sculptor's Assistant on some television shows including TNT's Snowpiercer.

Apart from working on art, I enjoy running, swimming, and spending time with my dogs Asha and Kira.
